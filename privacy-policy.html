Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to TripLanka. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, informative experience for all our visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information through our website, triplanka.lk.

Data Collection

TripLanka is primarily an informational travel guide. We do not collect personal identifying information about you unless you choose to provide that information to us through our contact form or by messaging us on WhatsApp.

We use standard cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website to improve our content and user experience.

No Financial Transactions

Please note that TripLanka is an informational guide and does not handle financial transactions or direct hotel/tour bookings on this website. Any links to external booking services are provided for your convenience, and their respective privacy policies will apply to any transactions you conduct with them.

Security

We prioritize traveler safety and the security of your data. We use industry-standard security measures to protect any information shared with us through our communication channels.
