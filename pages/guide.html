Know Before You Go

Entry & Visas

Most travelers need an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA). Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months.

Cultural Etiquette

Visitors are advised to dress modestly when visiting religious sites, including Buddhist temples, Hindu kovils, mosques, and churchesâ€”shoulders and knees should be covered as a sign of respect. When visiting Buddhist temples, avoid posing for photographs with your back facing a Buddha statue, and always behave respectfully within sacred premises. Remove footwear when entering religious sites where required, maintain a quiet and respectful attitude, and follow any local customs or guidelines observed at each place of worship.

Best Time to Visit

Dec-March for the West/South; May-September for the East. Sri Lanka is a year-round destination!

Currency

Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). While cards are accepted in cities, carry cash for smaller towns and street food.
