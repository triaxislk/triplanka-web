Sri Lanka Travel Guide

Everything you need to know about the most breathtaking regions of the island.

Colombo

The vibrant capital city.

Best Time to Visit

January to March when the weather is relatively cooler and drier.

Top Attractions

  • Gangaramaya Temple: Iconic religious and cultural center.
  • National Museum: Deep-dive into Sri Lanka's history.
  • Galle Face Green: Perfect for a sunset stroll.

Transportation

Tuk-tuks (always use metered ones), buses, and ride-hailing apps like PickMe or Uber are the best options.

Kandy | Gampola

The Hill Capital & Spiritual Heart.

Highlights

Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, Royal Botanical Gardens (Peradeniya), and the stunning Ambuluwawa Tower for panoramic views.

Travel Tip

The scenic train journey from Colombo to Kandy is a must-experience.

Sigiriya | Dambulla

The Cultural Triangle.

Top Sites

  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress: A UNESCO World Heritage site and an ancient architectural marvel.
  • Dambulla Cave Temples: Stunning golden statues and paintings inside ancient caves.

Adventure

Try an early morning climb to Pidurangala Rock for the best view of the Sigiriya Lion Rock.

Nuwara Eliya | Kitulgala

Mist-covered mountains & white-water rafting.

Overview

Known as "Little England," Nuwara Eliya offers cool climates and tea estates. Nearby Kitulgala is the hub for white-water rafting adventures.

Activities

  • Tea Factory Tours
  • Boating at Gregory Lake
  • Trekking at Horton Plains

Ella | Badulla

The Hiker's Paradise.

Must-Visit

  • Nine Arches Bridge: An iconic colonial-era railway bridge.
  • Little Adam's Peak: An easy trek with breathtaking views.
  • Ravana Falls: One of the widest waterfalls in the country.
