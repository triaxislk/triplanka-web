Important Notes

  • Apply Online: Travellers are strongly encouraged to apply for the Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) before arrival.
  • Validity: The ETA is usually valid for 30 days from the date of arrival.
  • Exemptions: Children under 12 years of age are exempt from ETA processing fees.
  • Advisories: Always check your country's official travel advice and the Immigration Department for seasonal updates.
