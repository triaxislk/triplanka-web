Why Ella Should Be on Every Backpacker's Bucket List

March 25, 2026 By TripLanka Experts 5 min read

There is a reason Ella has exploded from a sleepy hill-country village into South Asia's premier backpacker hub over the last decade. It combines the raw, misty beauty of Sri Lanka's tea plantations with accessible hiking, a vibrant cafe culture, and cool mountain air.

1. The World's Most Beautiful Train Ride

The journey to Ella is half the experience. The iconic blue train ride from Kandy to Ella winds through lush, emerald tea estates, mist-covered mountain passes, and dramatic viaducts. Backpackers hang out the open doors, taking in the sweeping views of the central highlands.

2. Hiking Little Adam's Peak

Unlike the grueling pilgrimage of the full Adamâ€™s Peak, "Little Adamâ€™s Peak" is an easy, 45-minute hike from Ella town. The trail weaves through working tea estates and ends on a stunning ridge with a panoramic view of the Ella Gap. It is the perfect spot for sunrise or sunset.

3. The Nine Arches Bridge

Completed during the British colonial period without a single piece of steel, the Nine Arches Bridge is an architectural wonder hidden in the jungle. Backpackers gather on the hillsides and the track itself to watch the train cross this magnificent stone structure.

4. Ella Rock

For those looking for a steeper challenge, hiking Ella Rock is a rite of passage. The 3-to-4-hour round trip begins by walking along the train tracks before ascending abruptly through eucalyptus forests. The view from the top is unmatched.

5. The Backpacker Vibe

After a day of hiking, Ella town comes alive. The main street is lined with reggae bars, artisanal coffee shops, and restaurants serving both incredible local rice and curry and Western comforts. The laid-back atmosphere makes it easy to meet fellow travelers.

Helpful Tips

  • Weather: Pack a light sweater; Ella gets quite chilly at night compared to the coast.
  • Train Tickets: Book 2nd or 3rd Class Reserved tickets weeks in advance, as the Kandy-Ella route sells out instantly.
