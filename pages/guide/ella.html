Ella & Badulla

Scenic Vistas, Iconic Bridges, and the Spirit of the Highlands.

Ella: The Hiker's Paradise

Ella is a charming town nestled in the central highlands, famed for its stunning natural beauty and relaxed atmosphere. Surrounded by lush hills and tea plantations, it offers some of the most iconic views in Sri Lanka, including the famous Ella Gap.

The Nine Arches Bridge, also known as the "Bridge in the Sky," is a marvel of colonial engineering built entirely of stone and brick. Watching the train snake across the bridge amidst the morning mist is a truly magical experience.

Photo by Anton Lecock

Little Adam's Peak

An easy yet rewarding trek offering panoramic vistas. Perfect for catching a breathtaking sunrise or sunset.

Ravana Falls

A majestic waterfall steeped in legend, named after the mythical King Ravana. Ideal for a refreshing stop.

Demodara Loop

A unique railway engineering feat where the track loops around and passes under itself.

Badulla & Haputale: Beyond the Mist

Photo by Hendrik Cornelissen

Badulla, the capital of Uva Province, is a historic hub home to the ancient Muthiyangana Raja Maha Vihara and the misty Dunhinda Falls.

Nearby Haputale offers even more dramatic views. Lipton's Seat, named after the tea pioneer Sir Thomas Lipton, provides a vantage point where you can see across five provinces on a clear day.

Highland Travel Tips

Hiking Prep

Wear comfortable hiking shoes and carry plenty of water for treks like Ella Rock or Little Adam's Peak.

The Train ride

The journey from Kandy to Ella is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful in the world.

Best Time

December to March offers the clearest skies for those panoramic mountain views.

Diyaluma Falls

Sri Lanka's second-highest waterfall, famous for its natural infinity pools at the top. A must-visit for adventure seekers.

Ravana Pool Club

The first international standard pool club in Sri Lanka, offering luxury relaxation with stunning mountain views.

Flying Ravana

Feel the thrill of Sri Lanka's first ever mega-zipline at Ella, soaring over lush greenery with views of the gap.
