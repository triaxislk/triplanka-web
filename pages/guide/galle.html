Galle, Bentota & Hikkaduwa

Sun-Kissed Golden Beaches, Coral Reefs, and Timeless Colonial Grandeur.

Galle: A Living Legacy

Galle is a city that blends tropical allure with centuries of colonial history. A bustling seaport since ancient times, its strategic importance grew with the arrival of Portuguese, Dutch, and British explorers. At its heart lies the Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the best-preserved European fortifications in Asia.

Wander through its narrow cobblestone streets, lined with colonial-era mansions and boutique galleries. Nearby Unawatuna offers golden beaches and the iconic Japanese Peace Pagoda overlooking the bay.

Photo by Orkhan Sweden

Bentota: The Golden Mile

Photo by Mohan Nannapaneni

Famous for its wide, golden sands and calm turquoise waters, Bentota is the ultimate destination for luxury relaxation and water sports. It's the place where the Bentara River flows into the sea, creating a unique coastal landscape.

Whether you're jet-skiing across the lagoon or taking a quiet river safari through the mangroves, Bentota offers a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility.

Hikkaduwa: The Vibrant Pulse

Hikkaduwa is the southern coast's most energetic town, famous for its world-class surfing breaks and the protected Coral Sanctuary just offshore. It's a paradise for snorkelers and divers who want to explore vibrant marine life among ancient coral reefs.

Known for its legendary beach nightlife and artistic soul, Hikkaduwa draws a high volume of travelers seeking both sun-drenched days and unforgettable evenings by the ocean.

Photo by Charith Kodagoda

Galle Fort

Explore the Dutch Reformed Church, historical museums, and the famous ramparts at sunset.

River Safaris

Explore the Madu River by boat, visiting cinnamon islands and experiencing the unique fish massage in the serene mangroves.

Turtle Conservation

Visit the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery or the turtle rescue centers in Habaraduwa to support marine life protection.

Coastal Travel Tips

Sun Safety

The tropical sun is intense. Always use sunscreen, wear a hat, and stay hydrated.

Best Season

November to April is the dry season, offering calm seas and sunny skies for the south coast.

Getting Around

Tuk-tuks are convenient, but renting a scooter gives you the freedom to explore hidden beaches.
