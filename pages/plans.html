Premium Safety Hub

Essential safety masterclass for a perfect journey across the island.

CRITICAL

Train Masterclass

  • Door Safety: Never hang out of open doors while the train is in motion.
  • Selfie-Safe Zones: Only take photos when the train is fully stopped.
  • Bridge Hazards: Nine Arches has narrow clearances. Stay inside.
HIGH RISK

The Water Rules

  • Waterfall Spray: Rocks around Ravana are permanently slippery.
  • Sudden Depths: Natural pools often have sudden 10ft drops.
  • Flash Floods: If it's raining upstream, exit the water immediately.
ESSENTIAL

Mountain Mastery

  • Stay on Trail: Straying off trails leads to disorientation.
  • The Early Start: Start your hike by 5 or 6 AM for best visibility.
  • Proper Gear: Grip shoes are non-negotiable for Ella Rock.
RESPECT

Responsible Selfie

  • Statue Privacy: Never pose with your back to a Buddha statue.
  • Ledge Ethics: Do not risk your life for a photo on cliff edges.
  • Ask First: Ask permission before photographing residents.
LOGISTICS

Traveler Toolkit

  • Apps: Use PickMe or Uber for metered transport.
  • Connectivity: Get a local SIM for real-time map access.
  • Cash: Carry small denominations for remote areas.
HELPLINE

Emergency Hub

  • 1912: Special Tourism Hotline for assistance.
  • 1990: Suwa Seriya Free Ambulance Service.
  • 119: Police Emergency and immediate assistance.
