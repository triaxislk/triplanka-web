Beach & Coastal Paradise Signature Itinerary

The complete circumnavigation of Sri Lanka's finest shores

Back to Journey Hub

Marine Safety & Seasonality Spotlight

Experience the power of the Indian Ocean with these 100% verified protocols:

Monsoon Mastery

May - Sept: Head East (Trinco/Arugam Bay). Nov - April: Head South/West (Mirissa/Bentota).

Reef Integrity

Never touch or stand on coral reefs. Use reef-safe sunscreen to protect our marine biodiversity.

Whale Safety

Use boats that respect international safety distances. Never attempt to swim with blue whales without permits.

Sri Lanka's coastline is a diverse tapestry of golden sands, wild surf, and turquoise lagoons. This 15-day itinerary focuses on the "Blue Flag" candidate beaches—12 coastlines identified for their exceptional water quality and environmental management.

Duration: 15 Days

The Blue Flag Initiative

This route features the 12 beaches identified by the Marine Environment Protection Authority (MEPA) for international eco-certification. Expect high safety standards and pristine environments.

Day 1: Arrival & Negombo Sands (Candidate 1)

  • Arrival at CMB Airport. A short 20-minute drive leads to Negombo, famous for its wide sandy beach and historic Dutch canal.
  • Explore the Negombo fish market and enjoy a sunset over the Indian Ocean.

Stay: Negombo (1 night)

Day 2-3: Kalpitiya Lagoon (Candidate 2)

  • Logistics: 3-hour drive north to the Kudawa beach area.
  • One of the best spots in the world for Kite Surfing and dolphin watching (seasonal).
  • Experience the wild, untouched beauty of the northwestern coast.

Stay: Kalpitiya (2 nights)

Day 4-6: The East Coast Gem - Nilaveli (Candidate 3)

  • Logistics: 4.5-hour drive to Trincomalee.
  • Relax at Nilaveli Beach, arguably the most beautiful on the island.
  • Take a boat to Pigeon Island Marine National Park for world-class snorkeling.

Stay: Nilaveli/Trinco (2 nights)

Day 7: Pasikudah Shallow Bay (Candidate 4)

  • Logistics: 2.5-hour drive south.
  • Pasikudah is famous for its semi-circular bay where you can walk hundreds of meters into the sea with water only waist-deep.

Stay: Pasikudah (1 night)

Day 8-9: Arugam Bay - Surfer's Spirit (Candidate 5)

  • Logistics: 3-hour drive south.
  • Arugam Bay is Sri Lanka's premier surf point and a top social hub for beach lovers.
  • Evening visit to Pottuvil Lagoon for a quiet boat safari.

Stay: Arugam Bay (2 nights)

Day 10: Goyambokka - Tangalle (Candidate 6)

  • Logistics: 4-hour drive to the Southern tip.
  • Goyambokka Beach is a secluded paradise with turquoise waters and palm-fringed coves.

Stay: Tangalle (1 night)

Day 11-12: Mirissa & Polhena (Candidates 7 & 8)

  • Mirissa is the hub for Whale Watching. Famous for its "Coconut Tree Hill" viewpoint.
  • Visit Polhena Beach in Matara, a safe, shallow bay perfect for spotting sea turtles in the wild.

Stay: Mirissa (2 nights)

Day 13: Unawatuna & Hikkaduwa (Candidates 9 & 10)

  • Explore Unawatuna’s pristine bay and the nearby Japanese Peace Pagoda.
  • Head to Hikkaduwa for vibrant nightlife and the famous coral sanctuary.

Stay: Hikkaduwa (1 night)

Day 14: Bentota Gold Sands (Candidate 11)

  • Bentota offers high-end luxury and vast stretches of golden sand.
  • Ideal for water sports: Jet skiing, windsurfing, and speed boat river safaris.

Stay: Bentota (1 night)

Day 15: Mt. Lavinia & Departure (Candidate 12)

  • Travel back towards Colombo via the Southern Expressway.
  • Final lunch at Mount Lavinia Beach, the historic coastal suburb of Colombo.
  • Direct transfer to CMB Airport for your flight.

"From the wild north to the social south, this itinerary ensures you experience every shade of blue that Sri Lanka has to offer."
