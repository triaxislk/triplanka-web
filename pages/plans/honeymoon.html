Honeymoon & Romance Signature Itinerary

Expertly crafted 10-day journey for an unforgettable beginning

Back to Journey Hub

Romantic & Safe Travel Spotlight

For a perfect and memorable honeymoon, keep these local safety rules in mind:

Respectful Photography

Never turn your back to Buddha statues for photos. Be cautious of "Danger Zone" photography on cliff edges like Mini Adams Peak.

Train Safety

Avoid hanging out of open doors for "couple shots. " Stay inside for a safe and beautiful view.

Waterfall Hazards

Rocks at Ramboda and Ravana Falls can be extremely slippery. Never swim alone in remote pools.

Sri Lanka is a paradise for honeymooners with its pristine beaches, lush tea plantations, rich culture, and stunning natural beauty. This comprehensive 10-day signature itinerary is designed to create an intimate and unforgettable romantic experience.

Duration: 10 Days

Interactive Route Map

Click the markers to explore the route and locations.

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo (Bandaranaike International Airport - CMB)

  • Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) and enjoy a comfortable private transfer to your hotel.
  • Spend the day at leisure. Explore Colombo's scenic Galle Face Green and visit Independence Square.

Stay: Colombo (1 night)

Day 2: Colombo to Kandy

  • Take a scenic train ride or a private car to Kandy (approx. 3 hours).
  • Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic and experience its serene atmosphere.
  • Attend a cultural dance show in the evening for a glimpse of Sri Lankan traditions.

Stay: Kandy (1 night)

Day 3: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya (Tea Country)

  • Visit Ramboda Waterfalls for a romantic photo session amidst nature.
  • Explore a Tea Plantation and learn the art of tea-making with a private tasting.
  • Stroll through Nuwara Eliya, also known as "Little England."

Stay: Nuwara Eliya (1 night)

Day 4: Nuwara Eliya to Ella

  • Worlds Most Scenic Train Ride: The journey from Nanu Oya to Ella is a honeymoon essential.
  • Hike to Nine Arches Bridge and Little Adam's Peak for breathtaking sunset views.

Stay: Ella (1 night)

Day 5: Ella to Yala National Park

  • Visit the misty Ravana Falls in the morning.
  • Drive to Yala and join an evening safari to spot leopards and elephants.

Stay: Yala (1 night)

Day 6: Yala to Mirissa

  • Enjoy a final morning safari before heading to the south coast.
  • Relax on Mirissa Beach and enjoy the golden sunset.

Stay: Mirissa (1 night)

Day 7: Whale Watching & Mirissa Beach

  • Early morning whale-watching tour (November to April).
  • Indulge in a couple's spa session or a private seafood dinner on the beach.

Stay: Mirissa (1 night)

Day 8: Mirissa to Galle

  • Drive to Galle, a charming colonial town.
  • Explore the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort and its boutique shops.
  • Enjoy a romantic candle-lit dinner within the ramparts.

Stay: Galle Fort (1 night)

Day 9: Galle to Bentota

  • Drive to Bentota and take a private boat safari on the Madu River.
  • Enjoy water sports or relax at a luxury beach villa.

Stay: Bentota (1 night)

Day 10: Bentota to Colombo (Departure)

Airport Tip: Use the Southern Expressway (E01) for your final drive to the airport. It ensures a fast, 1.5-hour journey, giving you more time for a final relaxed breakfast in Bentota.

  • Final morning dip in the ocean before your comfortable transfer back to the airport.

Key Accuracy Tips:

  • Best Time:
    • West & South Coast Romance: December to April
    • East Coast Seclusion: May to September
  • Accommodation: We recommend boutique heritage bungalows or high-end beach villas for privacy.

"This tour plan offers a well-balanced experience, covering Sri Lanka’s most romantic destinations while ensuring a memorable and intimate journey through the island’s natural beauty."
