Honeymoon & Romance Signature Itinerary

Expertly crafted 10-day journey for an unforgettable beginning

Back to Journey Hub

Romantic & Safe Travel Spotlight

For a perfect and memorable honeymoon, keep these local safety rules in mind:

Respectful Photography

Never turn your back to Buddha statues for photos. Be cautious of "Danger Zone" photography on cliff edges like Mini Adams Peak.

Train Safety

Avoid hanging out of open doors for "couple shots. " Stay inside for a safe and beautiful view.

Waterfall Hazards

Rocks at Ramboda and Ravana Falls can be extremely slippery. Never swim alone in remote pools.

Sri Lanka is a paradise for honeymooners with its pristine beaches, lush tea plantations, rich culture, and stunning natural beauty.

Duration: 10 Days

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo

  • Airport to Hotel Transfer: Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo and get a comfortable transfer to your hotel.
  • Explore Colombo: Spend the day at leisure. Explore Colombos scenic Galle Face Green, enjoy some street food, and visit Independence Square.

Overnight Stay: Enjoy a romantic dinner at a rooftop restaurant with panoramic city views and retire to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.


Day 2: Colombo to Kandy

  • Scenic Train Ride: After breakfast, take a train or a private car to Kandy. The scenic ride will be filled with picturesque landscapes of villages, rivers, and lush greenery.
  • Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic: Explore this UNESCO World Heritage Site and experience its serene atmosphere.
  • Cultural Show: Attend a cultural dance show in the evening for a glimpse of Sri Lankan traditions.

Overnight Stay: Check into a cozy boutique hotel overlooking the Kandy Lake.


Day 3: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya (Tea Country)

  • Visit Ramboda Waterfalls: En route to Nuwara Eliya, stop by Ramboda Falls, a perfect spot for a romantic photo session.
  • Explore a Tea Plantation and Factory: Learn the art of tea-making and enjoy a tasting session amidst the lush tea gardens.
  • Nuwara Eliya City Tour: Stroll through Victoria Park and visit Gregory Lake.

Overnight Stay: Stay in a colonial-style bungalow or a luxurious hotel set in the midst of tea plantations.


Day 4: Nuwara Eliya to Ella

  • Worlds Most Scenic Train Ride: Take the train from Nuwara Eliya to Ella. This journey is often considered one of the most beautiful train rides in the world.
  • Nine Arches Bridge & Little Adams Peak: Hike to the Nine Arches Bridge and then to Little Adams Peak for breathtaking views.
  • Ella Rock Hike: Optional activity for adventurous couples.

Overnight Stay: Book a boutique resort with stunning valley views.


Day 5: Ella to Yala National Park

  • Ravana Falls: Visit this beautiful waterfall early in the morning.
  • Transfer to Yala: Drive to Yala National Park.
  • Evening Safari: Go on a jeep safari in Yala to spot leopards, elephants, and other wildlife.

Overnight Stay: Stay at a luxury-tented camp or a safari lodge for a unique experience.


Day 6: Yala to Mirissa

  • Morning Safari: Optional early morning safari to catch a glimpse of animals in their natural habitat.
  • Transfer to Mirissa: After breakfast, leave for Mirissa.
  • Relax on the Beach: Spend the afternoon relaxing on the beach and enjoy the sunset.

Overnight Stay: Book a beachside resort or villa.


Day 7: Whale Watching & Mirissa Beach

  • Whale Watching Tour: Head out early morning for a whale-watching tour (seasonal from November to April).
  • Relax and Rejuvenate: After the tour, spend the day on the beautiful Mirissa Beach or indulge in a couples spa session.

Overnight Stay: Continue your stay at the beach resort.


Day 8: Mirissa to Galle

  • Transfer to Galle: Drive to Galle, a charming colonial town.
  • Galle Fort Exploration: Wander through the narrow streets, visit boutique shops, and explore the ramparts of the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort.
  • Romantic Dinner: Have a candle-lit dinner at a quaint restaurant within the fort.

Overnight Stay: Stay in a heritage hotel within Galle Fort.


Day 9: Galle to Bentota

  • Transfer to Bentota: Drive along the beautiful coastal road to Bentota.
  • Madu River Safari: Take a boat ride on the Madu River, spotting wildlife and visiting small islets.
  • Water Sports: Try water sports like jet skiing, snorkeling, or diving.

Overnight Stay: Stay at a beach resort or luxury villa.


Day 10: Bentota to Colombo (Departure)

  • Leisure Time in Bentota: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and some beach time.
  • Transfer to Colombo Airport: Drive back to Colombo for your return flight.


Additional Tips:

  • Best Time to Visit: December to March is the best time for the west and south coasts, while May to September is ideal for the east coast.
  • Accommodation: Book boutique hotels, luxury resorts, or heritage bungalows to make your stay extra special.
  • Transportation: Choose private cars for comfort, especially for intercity transfers, or opt for scenic train rides to enhance the experience.
  • Special Experiences: Arrange for a couples spa day, private dining on the beach, or a hot air balloon ride for added romance.


This itinerary combines Sri Lankas cultural heritage, natural beauty, and serene beaches, creating the perfect honeymoon experience!

  • You can adjust these travel tips based on your interests and time availability!
